Mountain Lochan Algae 2002: Intro.

These samples were collected for me by Mark Megaughin while carrying out his MSc Biology of Water Resource Management research project.
The project was a survey of fish and invertebrates in high altitude mountain lochans in Scotland, carried out at the Freshwater Fisheries Laboratory, Pitlochry, in relation to the European Commission's EMERGE project.
