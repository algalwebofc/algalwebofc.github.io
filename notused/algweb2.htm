How to use this website:

1: The Index contains links to most images on the site, if you know the name of the alga you are interested in. It is annotated to give a brief, but not definitive, description.

2: To browse algae found in particular habitat types, follow the links below and check out the thumbnails. Species lists for individual sampling sites are available under 'Location'
Pages devoted to identifying different growth forms are under development here (WebKey)

These pages are optimised for a screen resolution of 1024x768 pixels: the images obtained when you click on a thumbnail are the largest available, up to the limit of this screen size.

N.B. When this website was first constructed, you could open an image in a new window, resize it and open another to compare them. This facility was stopped recently in most or all browsers because of the nuisance value of pop-ups. As webmaster there is litle I can do to reverse this.

However, it is possible to get round this as a user, with some browsers. I have not been able to check them all, but Firefox allows you to choose: right-click on the link, and select the option "open link in new window" The full-sized images will then open in a new browser window. Re-size it and you can then open more than 1 image at a time in order to compare them. Some other browsers may also allow this.
