Dr. John Kinross



 

This website was assembled while I was working at Edinburgh Napier University as Senior Technician for the
Aquatic Ecosystem Management, Ecotourism, and Wildlife Biology and Conservation postgraduate courses at Edinburgh Napier University.

Subsequent to that I worked as a technician at Heriot-Watt University in Edinburgh, assisting with research in the Nano Safety Group and supporting practical classes. I have now retired and algal identification is a hobby.

 
 

 

  
  Qualifications:
  • 1969 BSc Honours in Biology (Genetics) grade 2II, University of Edinburgh.
  • 1970 Diploma in Education, Moray House College / University of Edinburgh.
  • 1985 MSc Biology of Water Management (Distinction), Napier College of Technology.
  • 1991 PhD Napier Polytechnic of Edinburgh
